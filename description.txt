This dataset contains product reviews and metadata from Amazon, including 142.8 million
reviews spanning May 1996 - July 2014.

This dataset includes reviews (ratings, text, helpfulness votes), product metadata
(descriptions, category information, price, brand, and image features), and links (also
viewed/also bought graphs).

K-cores (i.e., dense subsets): These data have been reduced to extract the k-core, such
that each of the remaining users and items have k reviews each.
